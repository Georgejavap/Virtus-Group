Events

We are excited to present these challenging yet rewarding events through our partners ULTRA X, HYROX and 220 TRIATHLON.

These events are designed to push your limits, enhance your fitness, and provide a platform for you to showcase your hard work and dedication!

Hyrox Championships

HYROX is the world’s first fitness race for every body — combining functional strength training with endurance in a competitive indoor environment. Whether you're a first-time racer or a seasoned athlete, HYROX challenges you to test your limits on a global stage.

Lift. Run. Dominate.

Each HYROX event features standardized workouts across 8 functional fitness stations, including sled pushes, rowing, wall balls, and more — giving athletes a true test of strength and stamina in a head-to-head format.

Power Meets Endurance

What makes HYROX unique is its accessibility. The event structure is designed to accommodate all levels, offering solo, doubles, and relay divisions. Train hard, compete fair, and feel the electrifying energy of thousands pushing together.

The Ultimate Fitness Race

HYROX isn't just a race — it’s a movement. With a global community of fitness enthusiasts, live DJ events, and elite athlete heats, the experience delivers adrenaline and achievement in equal measure. This is fitness racing, redefined.

Ultra X Championships

Ultra X is the ultimate test of endurance — a global series of multi-stage ultra marathons set in some of the most breathtaking and challenging environments on Earth. From the deserts of Jordan to the jungles of Sri Lanka, these races push runners beyond the ordinary.

Race Across the World’s Wildest Landscapes

Each Ultra X race spans 2 to 5 days, covering up to 250km of remote terrain. Runners complete a set distance each day, often camping overnight at race villages. It’s more than a race — it’s an immersive adventure in resilience and mental strength.

Where the Brave Push Boundaries

Whether you’re an experienced ultra runner or chasing your first multi-day finish, Ultra X welcomes all levels. With options to run solo or in teams, the events are built to challenge you — but also to support you every step of the way.

Endurance Redefined

Ultra X is more than a finish line. It’s a global community of explorers, united by grit and a love for pushing boundaries. With international participants, stunning backdrops, and unforgettable camaraderie, it’s an experience that redefines what you're capable of.

220 Triathlon World Championships

220 Triathlon is the go-to platform for triathletes of all levels — from first-timers to elite competitors. As the UK's leading triathlon magazine and media brand, 220 brings the best of training insights, gear reviews, race coverage, and expert advice to help you reach your multisport goals.

Push Past the Limits. Be 220.

Whether you're fine-tuning your swim stroke, climbing hills on the bike, or shaving seconds off your 10K split, 220 Triathlon equips you with cutting-edge training plans and performance tips from world-class coaches and athletes. It’s your ultimate resource to race smarter and stronger.

Swim. Bike. Run. Conquer.

From wetsuits to carbon bikes, running shoes to smartwatches — if it matters to a triathlete, 220 has tested it. With in-depth gear reviews, comparison guides, and buying advice, you’ll have all the tools needed to invest in the right kit with confidence.

Train Hard. Race Harder.

More than just a magazine, 220 Triathlon is a thriving community of multisport athletes united by the drive to push limits. With coverage of the UK's biggest triathlon events, interviews with inspirational racers, and a strong presence online, 220 connects you to the heart of the sport.
